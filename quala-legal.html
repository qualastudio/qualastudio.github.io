Privacy Policy

At Quala Studio, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. We comply with the Florida Information Protection Act (FIPA) and applicable federal regulations.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you:

  • Fill out our contact or quote forms (name, business name, email, phone, address)
  • Sign a service agreement with us
  • Communicate with us via email or WhatsApp
  • Grant us access to your business accounts to deliver our services

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Deliver the digital services you hired us for
  • Communicate with you about your project
  • Send invoices and process payments
  • Comply with legal obligations

🔒 We never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Ever.

3. Account Access

To deliver our services, we may request access to your business accounts (WhatsApp Business, Instagram, email platforms, etc.). This access is:

  • Always authorized by you through official platform APIs — never through shared passwords
  • Limited to what is strictly necessary to configure your systems
  • Revocable by you at any time directly through the platform settings
  • Never used to read, store, or share your private messages

4. Data Storage & Security

Your information is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use trusted platforms including Google Workspace, HubSpot, and Zoho — all of which maintain their own robust security certifications.

In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you within 30 days as required by Florida law (FIPA, §501.171).

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary:

  • While you are an active client of Quala Studio
  • Until the end of our service agreement
  • For a maximum of 2 years after our last interaction, after which your data is permanently deleted

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request corrections to inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke access to any connected accounts at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@qualastudio.com and we will respond within 10 business days.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are designed for businesses and are not directed at individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

Data & Security

We understand that giving a service provider access to your business accounts requires trust. Here is exactly how we handle your data and protect your security.

1. How We Access Your Accounts

We never ask for your passwords. All account connections are made through official API integrations — the same technology used by apps you already trust (like logging in with Google). This means:

  • You authorize access through the platform's own security system
  • You can revoke our access at any time directly from your account settings
  • We only see what is necessary to configure your systems
  • We never read, copy, or store your personal messages

2. Platforms We Use

All tools used by Quala Studio maintain industry-standard security certifications:

  • Google Workspace — SOC 2, ISO 27001 certified
  • HubSpot — SOC 2 Type II certified
  • Zoho — ISO 27001, SOC 2 certified
  • Meta (WhatsApp/Instagram) — Official Business API only
  • Calendly — SOC 2 Type II certified

3. Your Clients' Data

Data collected through systems we configure (leads, appointments, contact info) belongs entirely to you. We do not access, use, or analyze your clients' data beyond what is necessary to maintain your systems. We never share your clients' information with any third party.

4. Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a security incident affecting your data, we will notify you within 30 days as required by Florida's Information Protection Act (FIPA §501.171). We maintain an incident response plan and will provide full transparency about any breach and the steps taken to address it.

5. Data Deletion

Upon cancellation of services or upon your request, we will:

  • Revoke all access to your connected accounts
  • Delete your business data from our systems within 30 days
  • Provide confirmation of deletion upon request

As required by Florida law, we never retain personal data for more than 2 years after our last interaction.

🔒 Your trust is the foundation of our business. If you ever have concerns about how your data is being handled, contact us immediately at hello@qualastudio.com — we commit to responding within 24 hours.
